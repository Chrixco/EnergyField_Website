Game Rules

Game Overview

Energy Field is a turn-based strategic card game where 2-8 players compete to create the most efficient energy networks using circuits, scientists, inventions, and energy cards. Victory is achieved by accumulating the most points through strategic placement and network optimization.

üéØ

Game Setup

  • Each player receives a starting hand of 7 cards
  • Place energy source cards in the center field
  • Shuffle deck and place draw pile face down
  • Determine first player randomly
‚ö°

Turn Structure

  • Draw Phase: Draw 2 cards from your deck
  • Action Phase: Play cards and activate abilities
  • Build Phase: Place circuits and inventions
  • Energy Phase: Collect and distribute energy
  • End Phase: Discard excess cards (max 10)
üîå

Circuit Building

  • Connect cards through adjacent placement
  • Energy flows through connected circuits
  • Scientists can link non-adjacent cards
  • Broken circuits lose energy flow
üèÜ

Scoring

  • Connected inventions = 3 points each
  • Active scientists = 2 points each
  • Completed circuits = 1 point per card
  • Bonus: Largest network +5 points

Advanced Rules

Energy Overload

When a circuit receives more energy than its capacity, it may overload and damage connected cards.

Scientist Abilities

Each scientist has unique abilities that activate when properly connected to your energy network.

Tool Cards

One-time use cards that provide immediate benefits or disrupt opponent strategies.

Network Efficiency

Larger connected networks provide exponential scoring bonuses for optimization.

Players: 2-8
