The Story Behind Energy Field

In the near future, humanity has discovered a revolutionary form of energy that flows through specialized circuits and can be manipulated by brilliant scientists. This energy, known as the "Energy Field," holds the power to transform civilization.

As a master inventor, you must compete with other scientists to create the most efficient energy networks. Using your knowledge of circuits, the assistance of renowned researchers, and groundbreaking inventions, you'll harness the Energy Field to power humanity's greatest achievements.
